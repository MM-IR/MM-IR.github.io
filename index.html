Jianyu Wang is now a MS CS Student in UCSD, Jacobs School of Engineering. As valueable information, similar to the gem of a mine, distributed in all kinds of data, he is interested in Learning with Rich Experiences, especially the world knowledge distributed in our Internet, how to inject inductive biases into models learning from diverse signals, and how can agents perform reasoning over the huge knowledge base on-the-fly. He currently focuses on Natural Language Processing, especially attaching language to "extralinguistic events". He is open to academic collaborations and please drop him an email if you have a research proposal (problem/related work/evaluation criterion, etc). Jianyu is willing to help you come up with the idea to do creative, ground-breaking and enjoyable research!
